About Walkover

Walkover is a product-based tech company from Indore, India, building SaaS and AI tools that simplify business workflows.

Its flagship product MSG91 enables global communication via SMS, Email, WhatsApp, and Voice APIs.

Other products include viaSocket for automation and Giddh for cloud accounting.

The company focuses on automation, innovation, and scalable solutions to eliminate repetitive tasks in businesses.

Known for its flexible and engineering-driven culture, Walkover builds tools used by companies worldwide.
