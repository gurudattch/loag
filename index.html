About Walkover

Walkover is a product-based technology company based in Indore, India, focused on building SaaS and AI-driven tools that simplify business workflows and communication systems.

Founded in 2010, the company is widely known for its flagship platform MSG91, which provides enterprise-grade communication APIs including SMS, Email, WhatsApp, and Voice services used globally.

Walkover also develops products like viaSocket, a workflow automation platform, and Giddh, a cloud-based accounting solution designed for modern financial management.

The company emphasizes innovation through a strong engineering culture, focusing on automation, scalability, and user-centric design to help businesses eliminate repetitive tasks and improve efficiency.
